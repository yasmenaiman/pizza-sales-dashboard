#  Pizza Sales Dashboard — Excel

##  Project Goal
Analyze pizza sales data to understand revenue performance,
best-selling categories, and monthly trends using an
interactive Excel dashboard with slicers and dynamic charts.

---

##  Dataset
- Pizza sales transactions for the year 2022
- Features: pizza name, category, size, price, quantity, date

---

##  Dashboard Features
- **KPI Cards** — Total pizzas sold, total revenue, peak month
- **Total Amount by Category and Size** — 3D bar chart showing
  revenue breakdown across Chicken, Classic, Supreme, and Veggie
- **Monthly Total Amount by Quantity** — Line chart tracking
  quantity trends across the year
- **Top 10 Pizza Categories by Revenue** — Horizontal bar chart
  showing best-selling pizzas
- **Sum of Pizza Price by Category** — Line chart comparing
  total revenue per category
- **Total Amount by Pizza Size** — Pie chart showing size distribution

---

##  Key Findings
- **July** is the peak month for both quantity and revenue
- **The Barbecue Chicken Pizza** and **The California Chicken Pizza**
  are the top sellers by quantity
- **Classic** category generates the highest total revenue ($215,732)
- **Large (L)** size dominates sales across all categories
- **Veggie** category has the lowest revenue among all categories

---

##  Dashboard is Interactive!
The dashboard includes slicers for:
- **Category** — Filter by Chicken, Classic, Supreme, Veggie
- **Pizza Size** — Filter by L, M, S, XL, XXL
- **Date** — Filter by month
